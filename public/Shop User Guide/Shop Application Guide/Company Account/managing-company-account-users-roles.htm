Managing Company and Company Users in the Shop Application

The following article describes company account, company user and roles creation and management process.

Managing Company Account

Company management process starts with Company creation.

To create a Company in the web-shop, go to /company/register and register a company.

After the company has been registered, it should be approved in the Administration Interface: Company Account > Companies to continue building the hierarchy:

After approval, the user, who created the company, we will refer to them as to Company Administrator, can go to /company/overview page and create (and then also edit and delete) users, addresses, roles and business roles there.

A Company Administrator needs to log out and then log in to refresh the changes.

A link to Company Account is available in the header of the Shop App:

On My Company page, a Company Administrator can check company-related information:

 Managing Company Users

On Company Users page a Company Administrator can check the list of the existing company users, business unit they belong to, and the role they perform:

A single user may have several roles assigned, and all of them are enumerated in Roles column.

To create a new company user, Company Administrator needs to click on Create User button, fill in the required fields, and submit the data:

Any user with Administrator rights can assign multiple roles to a new user.

The following user actions are available for Company Administrator:

  • Edit - modifies the data for an existing user;
  • Disable - disables the current user account. Once the user is disabled, they will not be able to access company-related information:

  • Delete - permanently removes the user

On Users page a Company Administrator can invite users by uploading a CSV file:

CSV file has to be created using the following format: first_name,last_name,email,business_unit

 Managing Company Roles

Company Administrator can manage Roles in the Roles submenu in the shop application:

There are two predefined roles "Buyer" and "Administrator" after the new company has been registered.

To create a new role, click on Add new role button and enter the Role name:

Ticking Is Default checkbox means that the new users will be created with this role assigned by default.

A Company Administrator can either Edit or Delete the existing role. To enter the Edit mode, click on Edit button:

On Edit Role page, a Company Administrator can see a list of permissions that can be assigned / unassigned for a current role. By assigning / unassigning the permissions, a Company Administrator can control the possible actions for each company role within the company.
