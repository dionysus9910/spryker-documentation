Shop Guide - Business on Behalf

Your Business Unit page allows your customers to select company user account they are logged in.

To open Your Business Unit page, go to Customer Account -> Your Business Unit.

Here you can:

  • Select a company user to log in on behalf

Graphic User Interface

The Your Business Unit page consists of the following elements:

# Element Description
1 Customer Account menu A menu for managing your customer information, viewing orders.
2 Company User Account

A drop-down to select the company user account you are operating in.

The accounts are displayed in the format [COMPANY NAME / BUSINESS UNIT NAME].

No Business Unit value in the drop-down means that the user isn't yet logged in to any company accounts.
3 Remember Chioce A checkbox to make a company user account selection as a default one.
4 Submit Saves the changes.

Selecting Another Company User to Log in on Behalf

To log in to a different business unit, do the following:

  1. On the Your Business Unit page select the required company account from the drop-down:
  2. (Optionally) Check Remember Choice option if you would like to make this account your default one on login.
  3. Click Submit to save the changes.

After a successful change, you will be able to see [COMPANY NAME / BUSINESS UNIT NAME] instead of Your Business Unit menu item:
