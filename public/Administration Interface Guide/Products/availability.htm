Checking Product Availability

To check whether a product is in stock in the warehouse of the current store:

  • Click on View in the Actions column next to the corresponding product item.
  • Click on Edit Stock.
  • Select Quantity for the product and then Never out of stock if you want the product to be always available.
  • Click on Save.

If you created multiple variants, the stock can be managed under Product Variants for each variant.

To check the product availability for each variant of an abstract product can be checked under Price&Stock in Products menu.
