Product Lists

To navigate to the Product Lists, select Products -> Product Lists.

The Product Lists is the place where the Shop Administrators can define the product restrictions for a defined set of products by adding them to an appropriate product list.

The product restrictions work on the basis of whitelist/blacklist lists:

  • Whitelist: Products included to this product list type are always available for the customers.
  • Blacklist: Products included to this product list type are hidden from the customer view.

From this page you can:

  • Create a new product list to set product restrictions
  • Edit the existing product list
  • Export a product list
  • Remove a product list from the system
  • (Un)Assign a product category to a product list
  • (Un)Assign an individual product to a product list
  • Remove products from the product list

Managing Product Lists

When you start creating the product lists, the List of Products table begins to fill up. Each product list row displays product list details, such as, for example, product list type, merchant and business unit owner names the product list belongs to. See the description of each column in the List of Product lists table in the Graphic User Interface.

By default, the last created product list goes on top of the table. However, you can sort and search the list of product lists.

All columns with headers having arrows in the List of Product Lists table are sortable. I.e. you can sort the list of orders by product list ID, title, and type of the product list.

Creating a New Product List

To create a new product list, follow the steps described below:

  1. On the left side navigation pane, select Products -> Product Lists.
  2. Click +Create Product List in the top right corner.

    The Create Product List page contains 3 tabs:

    • General Information
    • Assign Categories
    • Assign Products

    The only page that has mandatory fields is General information.

  3. On the Create a Product List page in the General Information tab, provide the required information:
    • Title - fill in the title of your product list.
    • Type - select the type of your product list. It can either be a Whitelist, or Blacklist.
    • Owner Type - from the drop-down list, select the type of relationship the product list should work for. Currently, only Merchant Relationship is available as an Owner Type.
    • Merchant Relationship - from the drop-down list, select the Merchant Relationship you are creating the product list for.
    The Merchant Relationships are provided in the format: [Merchant Name] - [Business Unit].
  4. You can save the changes at this step by clicking Save and enter the other values for the other tabs later, or you can proceed to the next step - Assign Categories to a Product List.
Editing a Product List

To edit a product list, click Edit in the Actions column of the List of Product Lists table. You can change general information for a product list, such as Title, Product List type, Merchant Relationship, (un)assign product category or individual products to a list or, vice versa, remove them, and export a product list.
(Un)Assigning a Product Category to a Product List

You can whitelist/blacklist the whole product category in the Administration Interface. To do so, follow the steps below:

  1. Open the existing product list or create a new one following the instruction above.
  2. Move to Assign Categories tab.
  3. In the Categories field, start typing the name of the Category you wish to assign to a product list. The auto-suggested matching results are reflected in the drop-down list.

    Removing the Products from a Product List

    To remove a product from a product list, do the following:

    1. Open the existing product list or create a new one.
    2. Navigate to Assign Products tab.
    3. In the table Products in this list tab, find the product you would like to remove and place thecheck mark next to it in the Selected column.

      4. You can double-check the products that you are going to remove from the product list in the Products to be deassigned tab.

    4. Click Save.
