Product

Products are the core of your shop and deserve a great deal of your attention, as selling them is the whole reason behind your online store. Therefore, it is essential to know how to manage the products you present to your customers. The whole product management, from product creation to stock and product reviews management, is done in the Products area of the Administration Interface.

In the Products menu you can:

  • Create and edit products
  • Manage product availability
  • Manage product attributes
  • Create and manage product labels
  • Create and manage product options
  • Create and manage product relations
  • Manage merchant relation prices
  • Manage product reviews
  • Create and manage product sets
