Companies

To open this page, go to Company Account>Companies page.

At this page you can do everything a company user could do in the shop application to register a company, plus execute the actions that only a back office administrator can do, like approving and (de)activating a Company.

Specifically, from this page you can:

  • Create a Company
  • Approve a Company
  • De(activate) a Company
  • Edit a Company

Graphic User Interface

The Companies page consists of the following elements:

Creating a New Company

To create a new company, do the following:

  1. Click +Create Company in the top right corner.
  2. On the Create Company page, enter the name of the company you would like to create.
  3. Click Save. The newly created company will appear in the List of Companies table on Companies page.

Managing Companies

The companies can be managed by triggering certain actions in the Actions column in the List of Companies table on Companies page. Specifically, company roles can be Edited, De(Activated), Approved and Denied.

Approving / Denying a Company

Right after the Company has been created whether in the shop application or in the Administration Interface, it appears with Pending status in the List of Companies table on Companies page in the Administration Interface. A shop Administrator needs to approve the Company.

To approve the company, click Approve in the Actions column. As a result, the status of the company is changed to Approved.

If you want to disapprove the company, click Deny.

De(activating) a Company

Upon Company approval, a Store Administrator needs to activate it to make it visible in the shop application.

To activate the company, click Activate in the Actions column. As a result, the activity state of the company will change from Inactive to Active.

If you want to deactivate the company so it will not appear in the shop application, click Deactivate.

Editing a Company

Click Edit in Actions column if you want to change the name of the existing company.
