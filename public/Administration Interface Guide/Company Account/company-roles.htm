Company Roles

To open this page, go to Company Account>Company Roles page.

This page is where you can add and manage company roles. Generally, you need to create and assign company roles after a company and its business units have been created.

From this page you can:

  • Create a Company Role
  • Edit a Company Role
  • Delete a Company Role

Graphic User Interface

The Company Roles page consists of the following elements:

# Element Description
1 +Add Company Role This button adds a new company role. See Creating a New Role for more details.
2 Show [number] entries Here you can select how many items you want to see in the list of navigation elements (10, 25, 50, 100).
3 Role ID Numeric identifier of the role.
4 Company Name Name of the company the company role belongs to.
5 Name Name of the company role.
6 Actions In this column you can Edit or Delete a company role. See Managing Company Roles for more details.
7 Search Search and filter company users. Start typing a merchant relation ID, user name or company name to filter the results and display them by ID, Company Name and User Name in the List of Company Users table.
8 Showing [number 1] to [number 2] of [number 3] entries Shows how many entries ([number 1] to [number 2]) are displayed in the table. [number 2] depends on the value set in Show [number] entries (2), [number 3] represents total number of entries in the company users table.
9

Previous [page number] Next

[page number] is the number of the page you are currently on. Here you can switch to the previous page or to the next page.

Creating a New Company Role

To create a new company role, do the following:

  1. Click +Add Company Role in the top right corner.
  2. On the Create Company Role page, enter the required information: select Company from drop-down list and fill in the Name of the Company Role.
    Ticking Is Default means that the role will be applied to all new company users automatically.
  3. Click Save. The newly created user will appear in the Company Roles List table on Company Roles page.

Managing Company Roles

The company roles can be managed by triggering certain actions in the Actions column in the Company Roles List table on Company Roles page. Specifically, company roles can be Edited and Deleted there.

Editing a Company Role

Click Edit in Actions column if you want to change the details for a company role such as Name and set the role as Default.

Deleting a Company Role

If you want to delete an existing company role, click Delete in the Actions column.

You will be prompted with confirmation message:

Be aware, that you can not delete a default role. You need to set another role as a default one before proceeding with the action.
