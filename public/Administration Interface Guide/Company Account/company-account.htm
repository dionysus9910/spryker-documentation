Company Account

Company account is the main building block of the complex online B2B business model, as no B2B transaction is possible without a company and its related entities - business units and company users with specific roles. Even though company account can be set up in the shop application itself, it is also possible to create a company and its structure units directly in the Administration Interface. Moreover, after a Company has been registered in the shop application, it has to be approved by the back office administrator, which can be done on Companies page.

Go to respective pages of the Company Account menu to create and manage a Company, its Business units, Business Unit Addresses, Company Users and Company User Roles .
