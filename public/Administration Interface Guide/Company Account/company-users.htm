Company Users

To open this page, go to Company Account>Company Users page.

This page is where you can add and manage company users. Generally, you need to create and assign users to companies after a company and its business units have been created.

From this page you can:

  • Create Company Users
  • Edit Company Users
  • Enable/Disable Company Users
  • Delete Company Users

Graphic User Interface

The Company Users page consists of the following elements:

Adding a New Company User

To add a new company user, do the following:

  1. On Overview of Company Users page, click Add User.

  2. On Create Company User page:
    • Enter Customer's Email, Frst name, Last name
    • Select Company and Business Units from the drop-down
    • Check the checkbox for Roles you want to assign to the user. See Roles to learn how to create a new Company Role

      • All other fields are optional.

  3. Click Save.

Managing Company Users

The Company Users can be managed by triggering certain actions in the Actions column in Overview of Company Users table on Company Users page. Specifically, users can be Edited, Enabled/Disabled and Deleted there.

Editing a Company User

  1. Click Edit in Actions column if you want to change Company User's personal details such as Salutation, First Name, Last Name, Gender, Date of birth, Phone, Company, Business Units and Assigned Roles.
  2. Click Save.

Attaching a Company User to a Business Unit

To attach a company user to another business unit within the same company, do the following:

  1. Click Attach to BU in Actions column.
  2. On Attach to Business Unit page:

    • Select from the drop-down a business unit you would like the company user to be attached to.
    • Assign the role under Assigned Roles section.
    • Click Save.

Enabling/Disabling a Company User

If Company User is currently deactivated, there will be the Enable button in the Actions column. Click it to activate the user.

And vice versa, if user is enabled, they can be disabled here.

Deleting a Company User

If you want to delete an existing Company User, click Delete in the Actions column. You will be taken to Company user deletion confirmation page, and if confirmed, the user will be deleted from everywhere and will no longer be able to log in to the online shop.
