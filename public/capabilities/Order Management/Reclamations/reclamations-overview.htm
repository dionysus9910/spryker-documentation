Reclamations

Handling order claims is the reality of doing business for most e-commerce sites. A customer might have bought an item and then discovered, that it does not fit, or they might have stumbled upon another item and want to order it instead of the order already placed, or an item is faulty, or additional parts are needed etc.

The Reclamations feature is a simple, yet effective way for administrators to handle all the customer order claims from customers. Here the shop administrators can create, view, and edit reclamations in the dedicated of the Administration Interface. The editing implies creation of a new order, making a refund or closing the reclamation. Thus, the Reclamation feature makes the process of claims-handling easier, faster and more efficient for the store administrators, as well as helps to improve customer service.
