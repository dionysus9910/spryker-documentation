Products with Options in Shopping Lists

MC Multi-currency MLMulti-language MSMulti-store

When customers compile a shopping list of products that have options, they want the specific options to be added to the shopping list. Otherwise, if products are added without options, when adding the shopping list items to cart, they would have to go to cart and edit it by assigning respective options to products, which requires additional effort from their side.

The Products with Options in Shopping Lists feature allows your customers to add products with options to shopping list and subsequently transfer them to cart, just the way the product options would be added to cart from the product details page. Ability to add products with options to shopping list not only saves your customers' time and effort, but also urges them to use shopping lists more often, and therefore buy more frequently from your shop.
