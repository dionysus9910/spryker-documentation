Product Lists

To navigate to Product Lists, select Products -> Product Lists.

This page is where Shop Administrators can define product restrictions for a defined set of products by adding them to an appropriate product list.

The product restrictions work on the basis of whitelist/blacklist lists:

  • Whitelist: Products included in this product list type are always available for the customers.
  • Blacklist: Products included in this product list type are hidden from the customer view.

From this page you can:

  • Create a new product list to set product restrictions
  • Edit the existing product list
  • Export a product list
  • Remove a product list from the system
  • (Un)Assign a product category to a product list
  • (Un)Assign an individual product to a product list
  • Remove products from the product list

Managing Product Lists

When you start creating the product lists, the List of Products table begins to fill up. Each product list row displays product list details, such as, for example, product list type, merchant and business unit owner names the product list belongs to. See the description of each column in the List of Product lists table in the Graphic User Interface.

By default, the last created product list goes on top of the table. However, you can sort and search the list of product lists.

All columns with headers having arrows in the List of Product Lists table are sortable. I.e. you can sort the list of orders by product list ID, title, and type of the product list.

Creating a New Product List

To create a new product list, follow the steps:

  1. On the left side navigation bar, select Products -> Product Lists.
  2. Click +Create Product List in the top right corner.

    The Create Product List page contains 3 tabs:

    • General Information
    • Assign Categories
    • Assign Products

    Only General information tab has mandatory fields.

  3. In the General Information tab, enter the following information:
    • Title: Create a title of your product list.
    • Type: Select a type of your product list. It can be either Whitelist, or Blacklist.
    • Owner Type: Select a type of relationship the product list should work for from the drop-down list. Currently, only Merchant Relationship is available as an Owner Type.
    • Merchant Relationship: Select the Merchant relationship you are creating the product list for from the drop-down list.
    Merchant Relationships have the following format: [Merchant Name] - [Business Unit].
  4. Click Save and proceed to the next step - Assign Categories to a Product List.

    Alternatively, you can add information to the Assign Categories and Assign Products tabs later.

Editing a Product List

To edit a product list, click Edit in the Actions column of the List of Product Lists table.

This will take you to the page where you can change general information for a product list, such as Title, Product List type, Merchant Relationship, (un)assign product category or individual products to a list.

From this page, you can:

  • Return to the list of products lists by clicking Back to Product Lists in the top right corner of the page.
  • Export a product list into a CSV file. For this, click Export.
  • Remove the list. To do this, click Remove List in the top right corner of the page.
(Un)Assigning a Product Category to a Product List

You can whitelist or blacklist the whole product category in the Administration Interface.

To assign a product category to a product list, do the following:

  1. In the Actions column, click Edit List for the respective product list.
  2. Move to Assign Categories tab.
  3. In the Categories field, start typing the name of the Category you wish to assign to a product list. The auto-suggested matching results are reflected in the drop-down list.

    4. You can assign multiple categories to a product list.

    To unassign a product category, click X at the beginning of every category option:

  4. Click Save.
Assigning a Product to a Product List

To add individual products to a product list, follow these steps:

  1. Open the existing product list or create a new one following the instructions above.
  2. Navigate to theAssign Products tab.
  3. Add the products using one of the ways:
  • Import the products by uploading a CSV file.
    1. Click Choose File in the Import Product List area.
    2. Select the file to be uploaded. The file should contain product_list_key and a concrete_sku. For example:
    3. To save the changes, click Save.
  • In the Select Products to assign table, select the products that will be added to the list in the Selected column:

    • Use the search field to filter the products by entering a SKU or a product name.

    If all concrete products belonging to the abstract one are selected the entire abstract product is also selected.
    If an abstract product is selected, all concrete products belonging to that abstract are also selected.

    In the Products to be assigned tab, you can review all the products you have selected.

Removing Products from a Product List

To remove a product from a product list, do the following:

  1. Open the existing product list or create a new one.
  2. Navigate to the Assign Products tab.
  3. In the table Products in this list tab, select the products you would like to remove in the Selected column.

    4. You can double-check the products that you are going to remove from the product list in the Products to be deassigned tab.

  4. Click Save.
