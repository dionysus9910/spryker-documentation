Company Units

To open this page, go to Company Account>Company Units page.

This page is where you can add and manage company business units. Generally, you need to create company business units after a company has been created.

From this page you can:

  • Create Company Business Units
  • Edit Company Business Units
  • Delete Company Business Units

Graphic User Interface

Hover your mouse over the numbers to view their description.

The Company Units page consists of the following elements:

# Element Description
1 +Create Company Business Unit This button adds a new company business unit. See Creating a New Company Business Unit for more details.
2 Show [number] entries Here you can select how many items you want to see in the list of navigation elements (10, 25, 50, 100).
3 ID Numeric identifier of the business unit.
4 Company Name Name of the company the unit belongs to.
5 BU Parent Parent business unit the unit belongs to.
6 BU Name Name of the company business unit.
7 Address Business Addresses the unit has been provided with. See Company Unit Addresses to learn how to create a new company unit address.
8 IBAN International Bank Account Number that has been assigned to a company business unit.
9 BIC Bank Identifier Code that has been assigned to a company business unit.
10 Actions In this column you can Edit or Delete a business unit. See Managing Company Business Units for more details.
11 Search Search and filter company business units. Start typing a business unit ID, company name or business unit name to filter the results and display them by ID, Company Name, Business Unit Parent and Business Unit Name in the List of Company Business Units table.
12 Showing [number 1] to [number 2] of [number 3] entries Shows how many entries ([number 1] to [number 2]) are displayed in the table. [number 2] depends on the value set in Show [number] entries (2), [number 3] represents total number of entries in the company business units table.
13

Previous [page number] Next

[page number] is the number of the page you are currently on. Here you can switch to the previous page or to the next page.

Creating Company Business Units

To create a new company business unit, do the following:

  1. Click +Create Company Business Unit in the top right corner.
  2. On the Create Company Business Unit page, populate all required information: select Company from drop-down list and enter the Name of the business unit. Parent, IBAN and BIC fields are optional.
  3. Click Save. The newly created business unit will appear in the Company Business Units table on Company Units page.

Managing Company Business Units

The business units can be managed by triggering certain actions in the Actions column in the Company Business Units table on Company Units page. Specifically, business units can be Edited and Deleted there.

Editing a Business Unit

Click Edit in Actions column if you want to change the details for a business unit such as Parent BU, Name, IBAN and BIC.

Deleting a Business Unit

If you want to delete an existing business unit, click Delete in the Actions column.
