HowTo - Make a Product Searchable and Shown on Yves by URL

The article describes the flow on how to make a product searchable and displayed on Yves using a URL.

There are a number of conditions that should be fulfilled to make your product searchable and shown on Yves by URL. What is important is to make sure that your product meets the following conditions:

  • It is assigned to categories. See here for details on how to assign products to categories.
  • It is in stock in the warehouse for the current store. See Availability to learn how to check products' availability.
  • The product's status is Active. See here to learn how to manage products, including status change.
  • It has a price in the current locale.
  • It has been marked as searchable in the Admin UI.
  • It has product variants - abstract product will not be displayed on Yves unless it has product variants. See Products to learn how to create product variants.
