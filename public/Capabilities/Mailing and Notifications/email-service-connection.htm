E-mail Service Connection

Keep your customers updated with a variety of emails you can either send via the internal SMTP system or an external email provider of your choice. Automated emails regarding order status, shipping or newsletters are just a few examples of Emails to support the purchase process and increase brand loyalty.

Customers can configure the shop to use SMTP or sendmail. Alternatively, Spryker Commerce OS has integrations with mail providers that can be integrated into the shop. send emails via SMTP or connect mail provider. Email templates can be stored in the shop.
