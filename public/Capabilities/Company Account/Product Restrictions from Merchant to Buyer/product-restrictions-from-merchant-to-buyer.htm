Product Restrictions from Merchant to Buyer

It's typical that in the B2B business model a company would like to sell personalized products to some of their customers. And the company doesn't want the other customers to interact with these products. Both of these can be set up with the help of the Product Restrictions from Merchant to Buyer feature in Spryker OS. Providing clients with access to the products and information they need without overwhelming them with non-essential data is crucial for customer satisfaction.
