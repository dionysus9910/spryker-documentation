Under the Hood - Multi-Store Products

Database Schema

The Product module introduces a new table: spy_product_abstract_store. The table stores all abstract product-store relations. An abstract product is available in a store when it has a spy_product_abstract_store row for the given abstract product and store (regardless if it is a single store or a multi-store shop).
