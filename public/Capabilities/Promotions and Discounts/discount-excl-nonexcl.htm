Exclusive and Non-Exclusive Discounts

Discounts that have the Is_Exclusive flag set with value true cannot be combined.

If the cart includes more than one exclusive discount, only the discount that offers the highest discounted value will be applied.

If the cart includes a mix of exclusive and non-exclusive discounts, the exclusive discount that offers the highest discounted value will be applied.

If the cart includes only non-exclusive discount, then all of them are eligible to be applied.
